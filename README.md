## 👋 About Me

Hi, I'm **Geonwoo Kim**, an AI Developer and Undergraduate Researcher.

I am deeply interested in the synergy between perception and action. My main research focus lies in **End-to-End Autonomous Driving**, **Computer Vision**, and **Embodied AI (specifically VLA models)**. I'm driven by the challenge of building systems that don't just "see" the world, but understand and interact with it intelligently.

Currently, I'm a member of the **RILS AD Team**, where I work on practical AI challenges—from optimizing **data labeling/segmentation** pipelines to enhancing autonomous driving architectures like **SparseDrive** with **Mixture of Experts (MoE)** modules.

I believe in the power of turning research papers into robust, real-world systems.
